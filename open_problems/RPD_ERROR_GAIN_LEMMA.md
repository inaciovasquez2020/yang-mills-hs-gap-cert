# Open Problem: RPD Error Gain Lemma

## Statement
Prove that there exists \(C_{\mathrm{gain}}>0\) such that for all
\[
f\in(\ker\Delta_B)^\perp,
\]
one has
\[
\sum_Q \|\nabla_B f\|_Q^2 \le C_{\mathrm{gain}} L^{-1}\|f\|^2.
\]

## Role
Combined with
\[
|E_Q(U;f)|\le C\bigl(L^{-2}\|f\|_Q^2+L^{-1}\|\nabla_B f\|_Q^2\bigr),
\]
this yields
\[
|E_B(U;f)|\le C' L^{-2}\|f\|^2.
\]

## Downstream consequence
\[
\lambda_{\min}\!\left(\nabla^2S_B(U)\mid(\ker\Delta_B)^\perp\right)
\ge
\beta\lambda_1(\Delta_B)-C' L^{-2}.
\]

If
\[
\lambda_1(\Delta_B)\ge c_\Delta L^{-2}
\quad\text{and}\quad
\beta c_\Delta>C',
\]
then
\[
\gamma_B\ge (\beta c_\Delta-C')L^{-2}.
\]

## Status
Open.
No proof claimed here.
