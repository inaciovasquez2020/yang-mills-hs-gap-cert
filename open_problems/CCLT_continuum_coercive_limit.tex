\section{Continuum Coercive Limit Theorem (CCLT): Yang--Mills}

\paragraph{Statement (CCLT).}
For $4$D SU($N$) lattice gauge theory with Wilson action, prove existence of a continuum limit measure satisfying
Osterwalder--Schrader axioms and possessing a strictly positive spectral gap (mass gap).

\paragraph{Status / gap.}
Fixed-lattice reflection positivity and strong-coupling controls do not pass to the $\varepsilon\to 0$ limit;
the missing wall is global multiscale renormalization control that preserves an IR gap.

\paragraph{Closure target ingredient.}
A nonperturbative constructive RG theorem yielding a continuum OS theory together with a uniform (in scale) coercivity
mechanism that implies a positive gap in the continuum limit.
