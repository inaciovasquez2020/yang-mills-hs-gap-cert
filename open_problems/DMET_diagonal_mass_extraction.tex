\section{Diagonal Mass Extraction Theorem (DMET): Cells-Downwards RH Closure}

\paragraph{Statement (DMET).}
Let $M_T$ be the finite prime-power operator in the Cells-Downwards framework.
Prove that any subsequential limit of normalized spectral measures concentrates on the critical line and
excludes off-line mass via quantitative oscillatory suppression as $T\to\infty$.

\paragraph{Status / gap.}
Requires (i) a self-adjointness / spectral realization sufficient to force critical-line support and (ii) a
trace/explicit-formula control that yields vanishing off-line contribution with a quantitative rate.

\paragraph{Closure target ingredient.}
Essential self-adjointness on a natural domain plus a trace formula giving uniform oscillatory cancellation bounds
strong enough to prevent slow leakage off the line.
