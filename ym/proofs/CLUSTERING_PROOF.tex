\section*{Exponential Clustering for a Gauge-Invariant Operator}

Operator choice:
$O(x)=\mathrm{Tr}(F_{\mu\nu}F^{\mu\nu})(x)$.

Goal:
Prove or refute
\[
|\langle\Omega,O(x)O(0)\Omega\rangle-\langle\Omega,O\Omega\rangle^2|
\le Ce^{-m|x|}.
\]

Approach:
Spectral representation via OS positivity and transfer matrix.
Counterexample: construct admissible states forcing power-law decay.
