\section*{Explicit Gapless Sequence}

Construct $\psi_n\perp\Omega$, $\|\psi_n\|=1$, with
$\langle\psi_n,H\psi_n\rangle\to0$.

Constraints:
Gauge invariance, OS reconstruction, continuum limit.

Candidates:
Delocalized flux tubes, soft-gluon coherent states, topological mixing.
