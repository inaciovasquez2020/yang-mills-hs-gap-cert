\section*{RG Stability of $I(\Lambda)$}

Definition:
$I(\Lambda)=\inf\{\langle\psi,H_\Lambda\psi\rangle:\|\psi\|=1,\psi\perp\Omega_\Lambda\}$.

Goal:
Prove $\inf_{\Lambda\ge\Lambda_0} I(\Lambda)>0$ or construct degradation.

Method:
Compare transfer matrices across RG steps or exhibit a collapsing channel.
