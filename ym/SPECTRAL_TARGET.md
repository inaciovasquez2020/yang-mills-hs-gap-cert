Spectral floor target

Define:
- physical Hilbert space H_phys
- vacuum vector Omega
- Hamiltonian H >= 0 on H_phys
- orthogonality constraint psi ⟂ Omega

Target inequality:
There exists m > 0 such that
⟨psi, H psi⟩ >= m ||psi||^2
for all psi ⟂ Omega.

Uniformity requirements:
- independent of finite-volume parameter
- stable under cutoff removal used in docs/CONTINUUM_CONSTRUCTION.md
