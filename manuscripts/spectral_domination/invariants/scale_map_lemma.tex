\section*{SCALE-MAP (Scaling Normalization Lemma)}
The lattice Hamiltonian is normalized as
\[
H_{a,\Lambda}
=
\frac{g(a)^2}{2a}\sum_{l\in\Lambda} (-\Delta_{G,l})
+
\frac{1}{2g(a)^2 a}\sum_{p\subset\Lambda} (2-\mathrm{ReTr}(U_p)).
\]
All continuum-limit comparisons use this normalization.
No hidden $|\Lambda|$ factors occur in spectral gap estimates.
