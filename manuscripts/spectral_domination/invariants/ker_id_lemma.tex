\section*{KER-ID (Kernel Identification Lemma)}
Let $\mathcal H_{\mathrm{phys}}$ denote the gauge-invariant subspace.
Then
\[
\ker(E_\Lambda^2|_{\mathcal H_{\mathrm{phys}}})
=
\ker(H_\Lambda)
=
\mathrm{span}\{\mathbf 1\}.
\]
Consequently the spectral gap of $E_\Lambda^2$ on $\mathcal H_{\mathrm{phys}}$
is a gap relative to the true vacuum projection $P_0$.
