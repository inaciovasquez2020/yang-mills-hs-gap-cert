\section*{BC-COVER (Boundary Coverage Lemma)}
Let $\Lambda$ be a finite lattice region and
\[
H_\Lambda = \frac{g^2}{2a}\sum_{l\in\Lambda} (-\Delta_{G,l})
+\frac{1}{2g^2 a}\sum_{p\subset\Lambda} (2-\mathrm{ReTr}(U_p)).
\]
\textbf{Claim.} Every boundary link $l\in\partial\Lambda$ appears in the electric term.
Hence
\[
E_\Lambda^2=\sum_{l\in\Lambda} (-\Delta_{G,l})
\]
contains no boundary omission and admits the Casimir gap
\[
E_\Lambda^2 \ge c_G (I-P_0)
\]
with $c_G>0$ independent of $\Lambda$.
