\section*{BARRIER (Uniform Plaquette Barrier)}
Let $G=\mathrm{SU}(N)$. There exist a neighborhood $\mathcal N(I)$ of the identity
and $c_0>0$ such that for every plaquette variable $U_p$,
\[
U_p \notin \mathcal N(I)
\;\Rightarrow\;
1-\mathrm{ReTr}(U_p) \ge c_0.
\]
The constant $c_0$ depends only on $G$ and not on $\Lambda$ or $p$.
