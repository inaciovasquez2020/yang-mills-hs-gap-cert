\section*{Rigidity Functional}

For normalized gauge-invariant $\psi$ define

\[
\mathcal{E}_L(\psi)
=
\frac{1}{|\ell|}
\sum_{\ell}
\| E_\ell \psi \|^2.
\]

Define local fluctuation functional

\[
\mathcal{F}_L(\psi)
=
\frac{1}{|x|}
\sum_x
\mathrm{Var}_{B_R(x)}(\psi).
\]

\section*{Target Inequality}

We seek a constant $K$ independent of $L$ such that

\[
\mathcal{F}_L(\psi)
\le
K \mathcal{E}_L(\psi)
\quad \forall \psi \perp \Omega_L.
\]

\bigskip

If true, uniform spectral gap follows.

\bigskip
