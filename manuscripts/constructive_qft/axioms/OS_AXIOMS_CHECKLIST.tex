\section*{Osterwalder--Schrader Axioms Checklist (Target)}

(OS0) Regularity: $S_n$ tempered / suitable continuity on test functions.

(OS1) Euclidean invariance: $S_n(\tau_{g}\mathcal O_1,\dots,\tau_{g}\mathcal O_n)=S_n(\mathcal O_1,\dots,\mathcal O_n)$.

(OS2) Symmetry: $S_n$ symmetric under permutation.

(OS3) Reflection positivity: for reflection $\theta$ and any finite family $\{F_i\}$ supported in $t\ge 0$,
the matrix $M_{ij}:=\mathbb E[F_i\,\theta(F_j)]$ is positive semidefinite.

(OS4) Cluster: connected correlations vanish at large separation.

(OS5) Continuity: continuity in positions / smearing parameters.
