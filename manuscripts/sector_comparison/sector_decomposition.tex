\section*{Sector Decomposition of Transfer Hamiltonian}

Let $\mathcal{H}$ be the Hilbert space.

Decompose:
\[
\mathcal{H}
=
\overline{
\bigcup_{R>0}
\mathcal{H}_R
}
\]
where
\[
\mathcal{H}_R
=
\overline{\{ f(H)\Pi_R \Omega \}}.
\]

Question:

Is
\[
\sigma(H)\setminus\{0\}
=
\overline{
\bigcup_R
\sigma(H|_{\mathcal{H}_R})
} ?
\]
