\section*{Area Law Route Status}

Area law implies

\[
E_0(R) \ge \sigma R.
\]

However, mass gap requires

\[
\inf_R E_0(R) > 0.
\]

The missing step is a sector-comparison identity.

Without a new operator theorem,
the area-law route is incomplete.
