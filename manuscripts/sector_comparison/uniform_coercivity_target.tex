\section*{Uniform Coercivity Target}

Let $H$ be the Yang–Mills transfer Hamiltonian.

Mass gap is equivalent to:

\[
\exists c>0 :
\quad
\langle \psi, H \psi \rangle
\ge
c \|\psi\|^2
\quad
\forall \psi \perp \Omega.
\]

Equivalent formulation:

\[
\lambda_1(H) > 0.
\]

Reduction:

Find a uniform Poincaré-type inequality
on the gauge-invariant configuration space
that survives continuum scaling.

Obstacle:

No known uniform constant in 4D non-Abelian case.
