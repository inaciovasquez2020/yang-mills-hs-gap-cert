\begin{proof}[Proof of IMS Localization]
Let $\{\chi_k\}$ be a smooth partition of unity subordinate to blocks at scale $j$,
$\sum_k \chi_k^2=1$.
Then
\[
P_j = \sum_k \chi_k P_j \chi_k - \sum_k |\nabla \chi_k|^2
\]
as quadratic forms.
For any $\psi$,
\[
\langle \psi,P_j\psi\rangle
= \sum_k \langle \chi_k\psi,P_j\chi_k\psi\rangle
- \sum_k \|\nabla\chi_k\,\psi\|^2.
\]
Since $\|\nabla\chi_k\|_\infty\le C\ell_j^{-1}$,
\[
\sum_k \|\nabla\chi_k\,\psi\|^2 \le C\,\ell_j^{-2}\|\psi\|^2
= C\,2^{-2j}\|\psi\|^2.
\]
\end{proof}
