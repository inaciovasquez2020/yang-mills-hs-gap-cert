# Plaquette Spectral Gap Lemma

Let \(L_p\) denote the local plaquette Laplacian acting on a single plaquette degree of freedom.

Required statement:

\[
\lambda_1(L_p) \ge c > 0
\]

with \(c\) independent of the ambient volume.

Interpretation:

- the local plaquette sector has a uniform positive spectral gap,
- this serves as the atomic coercive input for blockwise gauge-space control.
