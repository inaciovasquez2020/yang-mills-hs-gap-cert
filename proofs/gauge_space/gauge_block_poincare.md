# Gauge-Space Block Poincaré Inequality

Let \(P_L\) be the block-averaging projection on gauge configuration space and let

\[
Q_L = I - P_L.
\]

Let the gauge Dirichlet form be

\[
\mathcal{E}(f)
=
\sum_p \langle \partial_{U_p} f,\partial_{U_p} f\rangle.
\]

Assume the plaquette spectral gap lemma

\[
\lambda_1(L_p) \ge c > 0.
\]

Then blockwise tensorization yields

\[
\lambda_1(\mathcal A_L) \ge cL^{-2}.
\]

Hence

\[
\|Q_L f\|^2 \le C L^2 \mathcal E(f).
\]
