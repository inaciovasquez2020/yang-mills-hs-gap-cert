# RPD Error Gain Lemma

## Status
Target only.
No proof claimed.

## Statement
\[
\sum_Q \|\nabla_B f\|_Q^2 \le C_{\mathrm{gain}}L^{-1}\|f\|^2,
\qquad
f\in(\ker\Delta_B)^\perp.
\]

## Role
Combined with
\[
|E_Q(U;f)|\le C\bigl(L^{-2}\|f\|_Q^2+L^{-1}\|\nabla_B f\|_Q^2\bigr),
\]
this yields
\[
|E_B(U;f)|\le C' L^{-2}\|f\|^2.
\]

## Missing objects
1. exact block gradient norm
2. gain mechanism producing the extra \(L^{-1}\)
3. orthogonality-to-kernel normalization
