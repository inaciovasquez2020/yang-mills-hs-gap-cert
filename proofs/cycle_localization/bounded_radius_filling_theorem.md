# Bounded Radius Filling Theorem

For every admissible local cycle \(\gamma\), there exists a filling \(\Phi(\gamma)\in C_2\) such that

\[
\partial_2 \Phi(\gamma)=\gamma
\]

and

\[
\operatorname{supp}(\Phi(\gamma)) \subset N_R(\gamma)
\]

for some radius \(R=O(1)\) independent of the ambient volume.

This is the constructive form of localized cycle generation needed for the fluctuation decomposition layer.
