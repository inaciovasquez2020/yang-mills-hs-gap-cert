# Local Cochain Homotopy

Let \(C_0,C_1,C_2\) be the lattice cell-chain groups.

Define a local homotopy operator

\[
H : C_1 \to C_2
\]

such that

\[
\partial_2 H + H \partial_1 = I - \Pi_{\mathrm{cycle}},
\]

where \(\Pi_{\mathrm{cycle}}\) denotes projection onto the cycle subspace.

Interpretation:

- \(H\) produces a local plaquette filling for the non-harmonic part of an edge chain,
- locality means the support of \(H(c)\) is contained in a bounded neighborhood of the support of \(c\).
