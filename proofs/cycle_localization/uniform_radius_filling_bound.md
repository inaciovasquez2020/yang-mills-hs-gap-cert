# Uniform Radius Filling Bound

Assume local contractibility of radius-\(R\) cubes and a local cochain homotopy \(H\).

Then every admissible local cycle \(\gamma\) admits a filling

\[
\Phi(\gamma) \in C_2
\]

such that

\[
\partial_2 \Phi(\gamma)=\gamma
\]

and

\[
\operatorname{supp}(\Phi(\gamma)) \subset N_R(\gamma).
\]

Hence

\[
\sup_\gamma \operatorname{diam}(\operatorname{supp}(\Phi(\gamma))) \le R.
\]
