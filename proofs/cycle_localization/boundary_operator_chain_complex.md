# Boundary Operator Chain Complex

Let \(C_2\), \(C_1\), and \(C_0\) denote the plaquette, edge, and vertex chain groups.

The boundary maps are

\[
C_2 \xrightarrow{\partial_2} C_1 \xrightarrow{\partial_1} C_0.
\]

They satisfy

\[
\partial_1 \partial_2 = 0.
\]

Thus every plaquette boundary is a cycle, and cycle filling is the problem of finding a preimage under \(\partial_2\).
