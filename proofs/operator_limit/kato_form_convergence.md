# Kato Form Convergence

Let \(q_L\) be quadratic forms with common dense domain \(D\).

If

\[
q_L(f) \to q_\infty(f)
\quad \forall f\in D,
\]

and the sequence is uniformly lower bounded, then

\[
\tilde H_L \xrightarrow{s.r.} H_\infty .
\]

This follows from the Kato representation theorem for closed forms.
