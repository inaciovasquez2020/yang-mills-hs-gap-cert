# Kato Closed Form Conditions

Let \(q_L\) be quadratic forms on a Hilbert space \(\mathcal H\).

Assume:

1. \(D(q_L)\) is dense in \(\mathcal H\).
2. \(q_L\) is lower bounded:
   \[
   q_L(f) \ge -C\|f\|^2.
   \]
3. \(q_L\) is closed with respect to the norm
   \[
   \|f\|_{q_L}^2 = q_L(f) + (C+1)\|f\|^2.
   \]

Then \(q_L\) defines a unique self-adjoint operator \(H_L\).
