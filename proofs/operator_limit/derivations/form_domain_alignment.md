# Form Domain Alignment

Let \(q_L\) be quadratic forms defined on domains \(D_L\).

Assume

\[
D = \bigcap_L D_L
\]

is dense and

\[
q_L(f) \to q_\infty(f) \quad \forall f \in D.
\]

Then the forms share a common dense core.

Thus the sequence of operators generated by \(q_L\) admits a strong-resolvent limit.
