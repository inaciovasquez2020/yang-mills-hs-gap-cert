# Strong Resolvent Theorem Application

Assume:

1. \(q_L\) are closed and uniformly lower bounded.
2. \(q_L \uparrow q_\infty\) on a common dense domain \(D\).
3. \(q_\infty\) is closed.

Then by Kato's monotone convergence theorem for forms,

\[
H_L \xrightarrow{s.r.} H_\infty.
\]

Equivalently, for every \(\lambda > -\inf \sigma(H_L)\),

\[
(H_L+\lambda)^{-1}f \to (H_\infty+\lambda)^{-1}f
\quad \forall f \in \mathcal H.
\]
