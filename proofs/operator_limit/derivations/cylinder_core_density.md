# Cylinder Core Density

Let \(D_{\mathrm{cyl}}\) denote the cylinder-function subspace.

Assume cylinder functions depend on finitely many local degrees of freedom and are stable under the finite-volume approximants.

Then

\[
\overline{D_{\mathrm{cyl}}} = \mathcal H.
\]

Hence \(D_{\mathrm{cyl}}\) is a dense core candidate for the limiting quadratic form and operator.
