# Form Convergence Reduction

For f in D_fin:

1. f depends on finitely many lattice variables.
2. For L sufficiently large, the renormalized local operator stabilizes on supp(f).
3. Therefore q_L(f) is eventually constant or eventually equal to a fixed local form.
4. Hence q_L(f) -> q_infty(f).
5. Uniform boundedness follows from locality on the finite support of f.

Remaining sublemma:
identify the stabilized local operator with the physical Yang-Mills local Hamiltonian.
