# Compactness Extraction Scheme

## Sequence
Let \(f_L \perp \operatorname{im}(P_L)\) with
\[
\|f_L\|=1,\qquad
\sum_{\ell}|\nabla_\ell f_L|^2 \le C L^{-2}.
\]

## Rescaling
Define block-rescaled functions \(g_L\) on unit blocks.

## Energy bound
\[
\sum_{\ell}|\nabla_\ell g_L|^2 \le C.
\]

## Compactness
\[
g_L \rightharpoonup g \quad \text{in } H^1_{\mathrm{loc}}.
\]

## Limit structure
\[
\nabla g = 0 \quad \Rightarrow \quad g \text{ constant on each block}.
\]

## Orthogonality
\[
\int_B g = 0 \quad \forall B.
\]

## Contradiction
\[
g \equiv 0 \quad \text{and} \quad \|g\|=1.
\]

## Status
Compactness yields rigidity contradiction.
