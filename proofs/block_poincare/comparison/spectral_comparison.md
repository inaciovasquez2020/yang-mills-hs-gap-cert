# Spectral Comparison for the Fluctuation Operator

## Operator
\[
Q_L := I - P_L.
\]

## Target spectral bound
Prove
\[
\langle f, Q_L^*Q_L f\rangle \le C L^2 \langle f, (-\Delta_G) f\rangle
\qquad \forall f \perp \operatorname{im}(P_L).
\]

## Equivalent form
\[
\|Q_L f\|^2 \le C L^2 \sum_{\ell} |\nabla_\ell f|^2.
\]

## Rearranged lower-energy form
\[
\sum_{\ell} |\nabla_\ell f|^2 \ge c L^{-2}\|Q_L f\|^2.
\]

## Mass-gap interface
Substitute into
\[
I \le a\,T_L^*T_L + b\,Q_L^*Q_L
\]
to deduce
\[
I - T_L^*T_L \ge \kappa (-\Delta_G).
\]

## Status
Spectral reformulation of the block-Poincare step.
