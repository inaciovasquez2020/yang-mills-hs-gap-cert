\lambda_{\min}(Q_L^*Q_L \,|\, \ker P_L^\perp) \ge c_0 L^2.
\]

\[
\inf_{f \perp \operatorname{im}(P_L),\ \|f\|=1}
\sum_{\ell} |\nabla_\ell f|^2 \ge c_0 L^{-2}.
\]

## Compactness scheme

\[
\|f_L\|=1,\qquad
\sum_{\ell} |\nabla_\ell f_L|^2 \to 0.
\]

Define rescaled functions on unit blocks.

## Target contradiction
Limit function constant on each block and orthogonal to constants.

## Required conclusion
\[
f \equiv 0 \quad \text{and} \quad \|f\|=1.
