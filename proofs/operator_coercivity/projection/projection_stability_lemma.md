# Projection Stability Lemma

Let

F_L = V_cycle ⊕ W_L

with orthogonal projections P_{V_cycle} and P_{W_L}.

Since V_cycle is a finite-dimensional subspace of a Hilbert space, the
orthogonal projection satisfies

||P_{V_cycle}|| = 1.

Hence

||P_{W_L}|| = ||I - P_{V_cycle}|| = 1.

Therefore for every f ∈ F_L,

||P_{W_L} f|| <= ||f||.

In particular, projection onto the fluctuation complement is uniformly bounded,
independently of the cycle-space dimension.

Consequence:

If a coercivity estimate holds on F_L before projection, it passes to W_L
without loss of constants.
