# Product Spectrum for the Discrete Cube

Let \(B_L = [1,L]^d \subset \mathbb{Z}^d\) with Dirichlet graph Laplacian.

For \(k=(k_1,\dots,k_d)\) with \(1 \le k_i \le L\), define

\[
\phi_k(x_1,\dots,x_d)
=
\prod_{i=1}^d \sin\!\left(\frac{\pi k_i x_i}{L+1}\right).
\]

Then \(\phi_k\) is an eigenfunction of the Dirichlet Laplacian with eigenvalue

\[
\lambda(k_1,\dots,k_d)
=
\sum_{i=1}^d \left(2 - 2\cos\!\left(\frac{\pi k_i}{L+1}\right)\right).
\]

Hence the lowest eigenvalue is attained at \(k=(1,\dots,1)\). In the one-coordinate excitation normalization relevant for the gap layer,

\[
\lambda_1(B_L)
=
2 - 2\cos\!\left(\frac{\pi}{L+1}\right).
\]
