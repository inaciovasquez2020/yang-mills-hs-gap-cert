# Mass Gap Transfer

Assume finite-volume Hamiltonians satisfy

\[
\lambda_1(H_L) \ge m_0 > 0.
\]

Under strong resolvent convergence,

\[
\inf \sigma(H_\infty)
=
\lim_{L\to\infty} \inf \sigma(H_L).
\]

Hence

\[
\lambda_1(H_\infty) \ge m_0.
\]

Using the operator identification

\[
H_\infty = H_{YM},
\]

we obtain

\[
\lambda_1(H_{YM}) \ge m_0 > 0.
\]

Thus the Yang–Mills Hamiltonian has a positive mass gap.
