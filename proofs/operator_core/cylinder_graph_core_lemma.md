# Cylinder Graph-Core Lemma

Let \(H_{YM}\) be the Yang–Mills Hamiltonian on Hilbert space \(\mathcal H\).

Let \(D_{\mathrm{cyl}}\) denote the cylinder-function subspace consisting of functions depending on finitely many plaquettes.

Lemma.

For every \(u \in D(H_{YM})\) there exists a sequence \(f_n \in D_{\mathrm{cyl}}\) such that

\[
f_n \to u
\]

and

\[
H_{YM} f_n \to H_{YM} u
\]

in \(\mathcal H\).

Hence

\[
\overline{D_{\mathrm{cyl}}}^{\|\cdot\| + \|H_{YM}\cdot\|} = D(H_{YM}).
\]

Therefore \(D_{\mathrm{cyl}}\) is a graph core for \(H_{YM}\).
