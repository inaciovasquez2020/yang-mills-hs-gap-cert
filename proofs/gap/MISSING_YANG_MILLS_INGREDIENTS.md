# Missing Yang-Mills Ingredients

Source verdict: DISPROVED_AS_A_YANG_MILLS_MASS_GAP_PROOF

Minimal obstruction: A proof of the Yang–Mills mass gap requires a non-abelian Wilson/Hamiltonian/coercivity theorem with continuum transfer; the present script contains only a scalar torus surrogate.

## Missing ingredients
- YM-1: Non-abelian operator — Replace the scalar torus surrogate by an explicit non-abelian Wilson/Hamiltonian/Hessian operator on admissible gauge configurations.
- YM-2: Gauge-invariant coercivity — Prove a uniform strictly positive lower bound on the physical quotient after removal of gauge zero modes.
- YM-3: Reflection positivity bridge — Derive the positivity/coercivity statement from a bona fide Yang-Mills reflection positivity or transfer-matrix argument.
- YM-4: Continuum transfer — Prove that the lattice lower bound survives the continuum and infinite-volume limit.
- YM-5: Mass-gap observable — Identify a gauge-invariant correlator/observable whose decay is forced by the coercive lower bound.
