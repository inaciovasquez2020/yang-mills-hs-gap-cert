# Uniform LSI Target

\[
\mathbf{(L1)}\quad
\exists \rho>0,\ \forall n,\ \mu_{\Lambda_n,a_n}\text{ satisfies }LSI(\rho).
\]

A weakest sufficient subroute is the uniform Bakry--Émery bound
\[
\Gamma_{2,n}(H)\ge \rho\,\Gamma_n(H)
\qquad
\forall n,\ \forall H,
\]
or a uniform finite-volume Brascamp--Lieb inequality plus a strict convexity bound on the effective lattice action.
