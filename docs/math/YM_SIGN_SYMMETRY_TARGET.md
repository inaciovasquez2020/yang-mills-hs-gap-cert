# Sign Symmetry Target

## Status

Conditional.

## Weakest sufficient lemma

\[
\forall n,\qquad
(\phi_\#\mu_{\Lambda_n,a_n}) = ((-\phi)_\#\mu_{\Lambda_n,a_n}).
\]

Equivalently, for every bounded measurable observable \(F\),
\[
\int F(\phi)\,d\mu_{\Lambda_n,a_n}
=
\int F(-\phi)\,d\mu_{\Lambda_n,a_n}.
\]

## Immediate consequence

Applying the lemma to odd observables gives
\[
\forall H\text{ odd},\qquad
\int H(\phi)\,d\mu_{\Lambda_n,a_n}=0.
\]

In particular,
\[
\forall f\in\mathcal S(\mathbb R^4,\mathfrak g),\qquad
\int \phi(f)\,d\mu_{\Lambda_n,a_n}=0.
\]
