# Uniform Carré-du-Champ Bound Target

\[
\mathbf{(L2)}\quad
\exists C>0,\ \exists m\in\mathbb N,\ \forall n,\ \forall f\in\mathcal S(\mathbb R^4,\mathfrak g),\qquad
\Gamma_n(\phi(f))\le C\|f\|_{\mathcal S,m}^2.
\]

A weakest sufficient subroute is to represent
\[
\phi(f)=\sum_{x\in\Lambda_n} \langle K_{n,f}(x),U_x\rangle
\]
with lattice coefficients satisfying
\[
\sum_{x\in\Lambda_n}|K_{n,f}(x)|^2\le C\|f\|_{\mathcal S,m}^2,
\]
and to use locality of \(\Gamma_n\) to obtain
\[
\Gamma_n(\phi(f))
\lesssim
\sum_{x\in\Lambda_n}|K_{n,f}(x)|^2.
\]
